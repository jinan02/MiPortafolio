My Experience

Data Scientist

Centrale Danone | July 2022 – August 2022

During this 2 months long internship, i worked on a system to optimize stock delivery, of Centrale DANONE’s company, in terms of time and route. All this, using k-means-constrained algorithm to divide all the clients into different clusters, based on their location, their orders and the capacity of the trucks in charge of delivering Centrale DANONE’s products. After conceiving the clustering, we've generated , for each truck, a map that shows the route that he has to follow to get to the group of clients he’s concerned Furthermore, we've generated, for each cluster, an excel file that contain the exact time when each client will get their order. At the end, we gathered all the parts we worked on, in one simple web application, to allow people to benefit from our service and interact with our system.
